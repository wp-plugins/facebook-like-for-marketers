Fast and simple Integration with facebook to provide the like/recommend functionality on your blog.

== Description ==

Leverage the power of facebook and open graph to enable visitors to your blog to like (share) the pages on your site with all their friends.  You can customize various open graph elements including title, description and thumbnail image.  These can bve set globally or on a page by page basis giving you absolute flexibility.

Sizing and placement options ensure that it fits into your site theme elegantly.
